About Raja Lodge 🏡

Established in the heart of Ooty over 45 years ago, Raja Lodge is one of the oldest and most trusted stays in the Queen of Hills. For decades, we’ve been a favorite among families, couples, and travelers looking for a peaceful, affordable, and convenient place to stay.

Our lodge reflects a blend of traditional hospitality and comfort with modern simplicity. Whether you're here for a honeymoon, vacation, or weekend escape, we ensure a clean, safe, and friendly environment for all our guests.

With our long-standing reputation, central location, and personal service, Raja Lodge continues to be the preferred choice for those who want to experience Ooty like a local — with the warmth of home and the beauty of the hills right outside your door.
