About Raja Lodge, Ooty

Established over 45 years ago, Raja Lodge is a trusted name in Ooty for families and travelers. We offer traditional hospitality blended with modern comfort.

From weekend getaways to honeymoons, our clean and safe lodge is your perfect hill-station home.

Located centrally, with personal care and beautiful surroundings, we ensure you experience Ooty like never before.
